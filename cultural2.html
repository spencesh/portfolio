Introduction

Discrimination comes in all shapes and sizes, as its definition: "treatment or consideration of, or making a distinction in favor of or against, a person or thing based on the group, class, or category to which that person or thing belongs rather than on individual merit"("Discrimination"). This affects everyone in todays world, whether they are apparent of it or not. It takes place everywhere, on the internet, in the media, amongst people in different settings. It's not always an issue that is brought to mind, often it is ignored since it is almost a way of life, but it definitely takes place. Major discrimination issues come in waves, racial discrimination was a big deal in the 1950's, it has now blown over for the most part, besides the areas that are accepted subconsciously, and currently in this era the biggest discrimination issue is a form of sex discrimination; the social issue of same-sex marriage.
