Anticipated Effects

If the tidal wave can just keep building taller and taller, eventually with a lot of time and effort, the wave will come crashing down, and America will be on its way to eliminating the discrimination of marriage equality. Prejudices will need to be put aside and forgotten. The language will be different, the opinions will be different, and with the unitedness the laws will be different. It doesn't just stop with a law though, a law does not determine how people act and respect others. Society's view on homosexuality does not just change with a law that legalizes gay marriage. In the concluding lines of the song Same Love Macklemore preaches, "No lawâ€™s gonna change us, we have to change us, whatever god you believe in we come from the same one"("Same Love Lyrics") exemplifies the fact that progress has to come from society, not the government. The effects that these solutions can have on the world is ever lasting. The way society behaves, including the way they think, will be totally different than it is now. It is easy to see how the social issue can play out, just by looking at the results of discrimination of skin color. People of any skin pigment have equal opportunities as another, they all can do the same things. Its hard to look back and think of how it used to be, the fact that African American's couldn't go to "White" schools or ride the same bus as whites, or drink out of the same water fountain is astonishing. Hopefully some day discrimination of homosexuality can follow the path of this and beyond. It would be ideal for a day to come that people are not shocked that a woman married a woman or a man married a man, and that it was legal in all 50 states. And for a day, where everyone is accepted into their community regardless of who they love and want to marry. And last but not least for a day that derogatory words such as "thats gay" is not used in everyday language, for it to be eliminated from the media, song lyrics, the internet, and anywhere else imaginable.
